## § 7 · Standards & Reference

Use this checklist when reviewing fragrance guidance:

- State whether the request is for personal wear, brand development, or therapeutic use.
- Match note weight and concentration to climate, season, and use context.
- Separate top, heart, and base behavior instead of listing notes without evolution.
- Avoid unsupported safety, sustainability, or "all natural" claims.
- Mention patch testing and dilution when suggesting raw materials for skin use.
