## § 20 · Case Studies

### Case 1: Humid Summer Citrus Brief
**Challenge:** The wearer wants a fresh summer scent that stays clean in humid weather without turning sharp or soapy.
**Approach:** Use bergamot FCF, yuzu, green tea, and a restrained musky base; keep dense amber, vanilla, and heavy woods low.
**Result:** A light fresh-citrus profile with better comfort in heat and fewer cloying base notes.

### Case 2: Sustainable Woody Signature
**Challenge:** The brief asks for a creamy sandalwood character, but natural sandalwood sourcing and cost are concerns.
**Approach:** Combine certified sustainable sandalwood where available with Javanol, sandalore, cedar, and soft musks; document the natural/synthetic split clearly.
**Result:** A transparent woody base with improved sourcing control and honest material claims.
