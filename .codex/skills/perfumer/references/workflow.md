## § 8 · Standard Workflow

1. Clarify intent, wearer, climate, budget, and restrictions.
2. Choose the main olfactory family and supporting families.
3. Draft the fragrance pyramid with clear top, heart, and base roles.
4. Identify safety, sourcing, and stability risks before recommending raw materials.
5. Recommend small-batch testing on blotters first, then diluted skin testing.
6. Iterate based on dry-down observations rather than opening impression alone.
